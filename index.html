About

Baker Dynamics is a team based out of Troy, Michigan. They are full of passionate students who have interests in technology and engineering fields.

The team consists of 15 dedicated individuals who actively participate in robotics competitions and other technological challenges. They strive to apply their knowledge and skills to develop innovative solutions in the field of robotics.

With a strong focus on collaboration and teamwork, Baker Dynamics aims to foster creativity and promote STEM education among young minds. They continuously seek to learn and grow, pushing the boundaries of technology.

Join Baker Dynamics on their exciting journey as they explore the fascinating world of robotics and engineering!
